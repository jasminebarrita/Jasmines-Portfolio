Bio

Some background. I grew up in the central valley, in a town called Modesto and the surrounding area. Growing up I always had in interest in creating art to show to my family and most importantly my dad, who nurtured my enthusiasm from a young age. About me! In high school I was involved in competitive running, and I love all animals. (including my puppy Frank!) Though most of the art ive created up until recently has been fine art, I do find visual or digital based art more interesting. I hope to pursue my interest in creating using new mediums and platforms in the future.
