Twine Project Game: "Outbreak" For this project I decided to have fun with the idea of covid mixed with the basic premise of a show i'm watching at the moment, The Walking Dead. I tell a story in the first person of your experience waking up almost 100 years after covid has become a zombie like illness. The story is captivating, and forces you to make a decision you think is the right one.

Polution Meme Project.For this project, I wanted to spread the message of the importance of pollution to the youth of this generation. Memes are a perfect outlet to simplify concepts so that everyone understands the general message.

My Glitch Art Project. This image focuses on a city in Harmosillo Sonora Mexico. For this composition, I exclusively used images I glitched through the datamoshing process in the program Audacity. I believe in the final product you can see the meaning of the image change as your eyes move across the glitch. The original clear image becomes blurred in a transition from normal to the glitched mountain and cityscape.
